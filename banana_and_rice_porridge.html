Step 1: Rinse the rice under cold water until the water runs clear.

Step 2: In a medium saucepan, combine the rice and water and bring to a boil over high heat.

Step 3: Reduce the heat to low, cover the saucepan, and simmer for 18-20 minutes, or until the rice is tender and the water has been absorbed.

Step 4: Remove the saucepan from the heat and let it sit for 5 minutes.

Step 5: Stir in the mashed bananas and salt and mix well.

Step 6: Serve the porridge warm and eat as desired.
