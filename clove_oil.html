Salt and baking soda are both natural antibacterial agents and can help to reduce inflammation and pain in the affected tooth. The warm water helps to soothe the area as well. However, this remedy is not a substitute for professional dental care and if the pain persists or becomes worse, it's important to see a dentist.

Step 1: Mix the salt and baking soda together in a small bowl.

Step 2: Add a few drops of warm water to the mixture and stir until it forms a paste.

Step 3: Apply the paste to the affected tooth and surrounding area.

Step 4: Leave the paste on for a few minutes, then rinse with warm water.

Step 5: Repeat as needed.
