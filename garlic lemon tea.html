Garlic and Lemon Tea

Garlic is known for its antibacterial and antiviral properties, and may help boost the immune system. Lemon juice contains vitamin C, which is also known for relieving cold symptoms.

Step 1: In a small pot, add the garlic and water and bring to a boil.

Step 2: Reduce the heat and let the mixture simmer for 10-15 minutes.

Step 3: Strain the garlic tea into a mug.

Step 4: Add lemon juice and honey (if desired) to the tea and stir well.

Step 5: Drink the tea slowly while it's still warm.
