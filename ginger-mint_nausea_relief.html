Ginger-Mint Nausea Relief

The ginger in this recipe has natural anti-inflammatory and digestive properties that can help to calm the stomach and reduce nausea. Mint leaves can also have a soothing effect on the digestive system. Honey and lemon can help to add a touch of sweetness and acidity, respectively, to balance the flavor. This natural remedy is easy to make and can be a great alternative to over-the-counter anti-nausea medications.

Step 1: In a mug, combine the hot water, grated ginger, and mint leaves.

Step 2: Stir well to combine.

Step 3: Let the mixture steep for 5-10 minutes to allow the flavors to meld.

Step 4: Strain the mixture through a fine mesh strainer to remove the ginger and mint leaves.

Step 5: Add honey (if using) and lemon juice to taste.

Step 6: Drink the mixture slowly while it's still warm, as needed to help relieve nausea.
