Herbal Muscle Pain Reliver

This might be just what you need to feel better from muscle pain. The epsom salt and peppermint oils help cool down your muscles and make you feel better. Note: It's important to always dilute essential oils in a carrier oil or a base such as Epsom salt before use. Do not apply undiluted essential oils directly to your skin. Additionally, if you have any medical conditions or concerns, consult with your healthcare provider before trying any new remedies.

Step 1: Fill a bathtub with warm water.

Step 2: Add the Epsom salt and stir until it's fully dissolved.

Step 3: Add the peppermint and lavender essential oils and stir well.

Step 4: Soak in the bath for at least 20-30 minutes, allowing your muscles to relax and absorb the beneficial properties of the Epsom salt and essential oils.
