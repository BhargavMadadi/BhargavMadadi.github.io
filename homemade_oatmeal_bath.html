Homemade Oatmeal Bath

This oatmeal bath can help to soothe skin irritation and provide relief from itching, inflammation, and other symptoms.

Step 1: Grind the oatmeal in a food processor or blender until it is a fine powder.

Step 2: Mix the oatmeal powder and baking soda in a bowl.

Step 3: If using essential oil, add a few drops to the mixture and stir well.

Step 4: Fill a bathtub with warm water.

Step 5: Add the oatmeal mixture to the bath and stir well to ensure it is evenly distributed.

Step 6: Soak in the bath for 15-20 minutes, gently rubbing the oatmeal mixture onto the skin for additional relief.
