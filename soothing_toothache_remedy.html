Soothing Toothache Remedy

Clove and ginger have natural analgesic and anti-inflammatory properties that can help to alleviate toothache pain, while cayenne pepper can help to reduce inflammation and increase circulation to the affected area. Coconut oil and honey have natural antibacterial properties and can help to soothe the affected area.

Step 1: Mix the ground clove, ground ginger, and cayenne pepper in a small bowl.

Step 2: Add the coconut oil and honey to the bowl and stir until the mixture is well-combined.

Step 3: Apply a small amount of the mixture directly to the affected tooth and gum area.

Step 4: Leave the mixture on for 10-15 minutes, then rinse your mouth with warm water.
