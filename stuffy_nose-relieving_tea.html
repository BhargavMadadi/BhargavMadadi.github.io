Stuffy Nose Relieving Tea

This recipe is a soothing tea designed to relieve a stuffy nose. It includes ginger and cinnamon, which are known for their natural anti-inflammatory and decongestant properties, and raw honey and lemon, which can help to soothe and provide relief to the nasal passages. The tea is easy to make and can be consumed whenever you're feeling congested and in need of some relief.

Step 1: Bring the water to a boil in a small pot.

Step 2: Add the ginger and cinnamon stick to the pot and reduce the heat to low.

Step 3: Let the herbs simmer in the water for 10-15 minutes.

Step 4: Remove the pot from the heat and strain the tea into a cup.

Step 5: Add the raw honey and squeeze the lemon wedge into the tea.

Step 6: Stir well and drink the tea while it's still warm.
