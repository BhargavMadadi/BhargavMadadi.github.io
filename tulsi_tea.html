Tulsi Tea

Tulsi, also known as holy basil, is a popular herb in Ayurvedic medicine and is believed to have antiviral, antibacterial, and anti-inflammatory properties. Drinking tulsi tea may help alleviate cold symptoms such as cough, congestion, and fever.

Step 1: Boil the water in a pot.

Step 2: Add the tulsi leaves to the water and reduce the heat to low.

Step 3: Let the tulsi leaves steep in the water for about 5-10 minutes.

Step 4: Strain the tea into a cup and add honey to taste, if desired.
