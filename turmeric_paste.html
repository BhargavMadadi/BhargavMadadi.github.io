Turmeric Paste

The turmeric and black pepper in the paste have anti-inflammatory properties, while the coconut oil can help to moisturize and soothe the skin. This paste can be applied topically to the affected area, but it's important to note that turmeric can stain fabric and surfaces, so use caution when applying it.

Step 1: In a medium-sized saucepan, bring the water to a boil.

Step 2: Add the turmeric and black pepper to the boiling water and reduce heat to low.

Step 3: Stir the mixture until it forms a thick paste, which should take about 5-10 minutes.

Step 4: Remove the saucepan from the heat and allow the mixture to cool for a few minutes.

Step 5: Add the coconut oil to the paste and stir until the oil has melted and is fully incorporated.

Step 6: Transfer the turmeric paste to a glass jar with a tight-fitting lid and store it in the refrigerator.

Step 7: This turmeric paste can be used topically to soothe skin irritation, but please note that turmeric can stain fabric and surfaces, so be careful when applying it.
