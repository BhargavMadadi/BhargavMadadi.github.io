Warm Compress

A warm compress can help to relieve pain and reduce inflammation in the ear. The warmth can also help to improve blood flow to the area, which can promote healing. Be sure to use warm, not hot water, to avoid burning the skin. If your earache is severe or accompanied by other symptoms such as fever or hearing loss, it's important to seek medical attention.

Step 1: Soak a clean washcloth or towel in warm water.

Step 2: Wring out the excess water.

Step 3: Place the warm compress over the affected ear.

Step 4: Leave it on for 10-15 minutes.

Step 5: Repeat 2-3 times a day.
